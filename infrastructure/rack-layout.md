# Rack Overview
This homelab is housed in an enclosed rack. Servers and devices in the rack are placed in with prioritization of: 
- Heat Management
- Cable Management
- Accessibility for Maintenance
- Separation for Networking and Computing

### Rack Unit Allocation
The rack layout follows a logical top-down design and separation, making maintenance and accessibility straightforward: <br>
- ```Top```:```Patching and cable management```
- ```Middle```:```Routing/Switching along with Secondary Server```
- ```Lower```:```Primary Server housing```
- ```Bottom / Back```:```Power Distribution Unit ```
